**Quota** is a motivational application designed for those who believe that greatness is built through daily consistency. The philosophy is simple: Every day you have a **quota** of effort, learning, and discipline to meet. If you hit your quota, you win the day.

I built this app because I realized that whether I was training for the **Informatics Olympiad** or building my **HOME LABS**, the biggest challenge wasn't the code‚Äîit was the consistency. **Quota** is the technical solution to mental discipline.
